Our Story

Wood is a living
material

"Every grain tells a story. Our job is to make sure yours is worth telling."

Two Seven Woodworks was founded on a simple belief: mass production has no place in your home. We work with a small number of clients at a time to ensure every commission receives the full attention it deserves — from initial sketch to final installation.


Based in the Houston/Katy area, we source premium hardwoods and collaborate closely with each client to design pieces that last generations.

What We Work With

Premium hardwoods,
sourced with intention.

We work exclusively with premium North American and imported hardwoods — selected for grain, character, and longevity. Every species has a story; we help you choose the one that fits yours.

North America

Black Walnut

Rich chocolate tones with a straight, open grain. The gold standard for heirloom furniture — darkens beautifully with age.

North America

White Oak

Pale blonde with subtle ray flecks. Exceptionally versatile — pairs with modern, Scandinavian, and traditional interiors alike.

North America

Hard Maple

Creamy white with a fine, uniform texture. Exceptional hardness makes it ideal for cabinetry and high-traffic surfaces.

North America

Cherry

Warm reddish-brown that deepens dramatically with light exposure. A classic choice for formal dining rooms and heirloom pieces.
